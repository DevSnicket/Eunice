After working for nearly two decades in software development, I noticed a consensus on what people considered to be valuable qualities in software and how they can be beneficial to productivity.

The difficulty seemed to be in expressing and applying these principles, especially with consistency when working in teams. I realized there was potential to make a new type of development tool that embodied some of these principles related to cohesion, modularity, structure, dependencies and architecture.

After leaving my job I started working full-time on the new tool, called Eunice.
